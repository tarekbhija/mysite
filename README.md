# Tarek Bhija Personal Website

## Compact strategy summary

- **Recommended positioning**: Fractional Growth & Product Executive for companies where growth, product, eCommerce, and governance need to be connected into one execution system.
- **Ideal client profile**: Founders, CEOs, GMs, healthcare/pharma/medtech operators, consumer brands, eCommerce leaders, and scale-ups needing senior advisory or fractional leadership.
- **Recommended site structure**: Premium one-page website with anchored sections. This is the lowest-credit, highest-conversion option because it keeps the story focused and removes navigation friction.
- **Service offers**: Fractional Growth Leadership; Product & eCommerce Acceleration; Go-to-Market & Portfolio Strategy; Governance, Risk & Scale-up Support.
- **Design direction**: Minimal executive editorial style, warm ivory surfaces, deep ink text, restrained teal and bronze accents, strong typography, no dependence on headshots or stock imagery.

- **Secondary CTA**: View LinkedIn.
